Grading

Programming Assignments (7): 30%
Programming assignments will be assigned regularly. You have a total of four late days that you can use throughout the quarter (where a late day means anything between 1 second and 23 hours 59 minutes and 59 seconds past a deadline). After that, we take 15% off a given assignment for each additional late day.
Midterm (Open Book): 35%
Midterm exam will be held on October 28th. It will be closed book, but you will be allowed one cheat-sheet.
Final (Open Book): 35%
Final examination must be taken at the time and place scheduled by the registrar. It is currently scheduled for TBD. You must present a valid UCSD ID. The final is closed book, but you will be allowed on cheat-sheet.
